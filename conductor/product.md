# Initial Concept
A professional AI-powered video production suite that runs locally on your desktop.

# Product Vision
The AI Video Production Editor is a professional-grade, standalone desktop application designed to democratize high-end filmmaking and content creation. By integrating advanced generative AI models (like Gemini, Veo, and Imagen) directly into a traditional video production workflow, it allows creators to move from initial concept to a polished "Director's Cut" entirely within a single, privacy-focused environment.

# Primary Goals
- **AI-Driven Storytelling**: Automate the scriptwriting process using proven narrative frameworks (McKee/Campbell), ensuring every project has a strong emotional and structural foundation.
- **Visual Intelligence**: Generate context-aware image storyboards that reflect the emotional beats of the script, providing immediate visual feedback.
- **State-of-the-Art Integration**: Provide a unified workspace for high-fidelity video and image generation through seamless API integration (Google Veo, Imagen, etc.).
- **Automated Quality Control**: Offer an "AI Director" feature that performs automated critiques, consistency checks, and editing suggestions.
- **Local Privacy & Security**: Maintain a "Bring Your Own Key" (BYOK) model and ensure all sensitive data and API keys are stored securely on the user's local machine.

# Target Audience
- **Independent Filmmakers & Creative Directors**: Who need high-quality storytelling tools and granular control over the creative process without the overhead of a traditional studio.
- **Content Marketing Teams & Social Media Agencies**: Who require speed, efficiency, and professional results for high-volume video production.
- **YouTube Creators & Educators**: Who want to leverage AI for rapid prototyping, storyboard visualization, and accessible high-end production values.

# Key Features
- **Advanced AI Scripting Engine**: Deep integration of McKee and Campbell storytelling principles for generating structured, emotionally resonant scripts.
- **Contextual Storyboarding**: An AI system that interprets script beats to automatically generate and manage image assets for storyboards.
- **Unified Generation Workspace**: A seamless interface to toggle between, compare, and manage various AI generation models (Veo, Imagen, Replicate).
- **Automated "Director's Cut"**: AI-driven review loops that provide editing suggestions and quality assessments to refine the final output.

# UI/UX Philosophy
- **Professional & AI-Integrated**: A dark-themed, high-density interface reminiscent of professional NLEs (Non-Linear Editors) like DaVinci Resolve, but augmented with an "AI-First" approach where conversational prompts and AI agents are deeply embedded into the workflow.
