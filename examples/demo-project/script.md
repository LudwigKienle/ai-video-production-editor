# Rainline

Short public demo script for AI Video Production Editor.

## Scene 1

EXT. NEON MARKET STREET - NIGHT

Rain turns the street into a mirror. A delivery rider, MIRA VALE, 27, pushes
her bike beneath a flickering noodle shop sign. Her yellow rain jacket is torn
at the left sleeve. A small silver compass charm hangs from her zipper.

Her phone buzzes with a delivery timer: 00:59.

Mira hears a metallic tapping from the storm drain.

She kneels. Between the bars, a black memory device pulses with a weak blue
light.

MIRA
Nobody drops tech in the rain by accident.

She reaches down, pulls the device free, and wipes mud from its glass surface.
The screen wakes.

ON DEVICE SCREEN:

Mira sees herself standing in the same street one minute from now. The noodle
shop sign is dark. Her bike is gone. Someone behind her whispers her name.

BACK TO STREET:

The delivery timer hits 00:30.

A city bus rushes past. For one frame, its window reflection shows a tall figure
in a clear plastic coat watching Mira from across the street.

Mira turns. No one is there.

The device flashes a new message:

`RETURN WHAT YOU STOLE.`

Mira looks at her delivery bag. The zipper is open. Inside is a sealed white
envelope she has never seen before.

The noodle shop sign flickers out.

MIRA
Okay. Tomorrow can wait.

She grabs the envelope, pockets the device, and runs into the rain.

CUT TO BLACK.
