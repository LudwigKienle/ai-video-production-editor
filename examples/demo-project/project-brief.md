# Rainline Project Brief

## Format

- Length: 30 to 60 seconds.
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 for desktop demo, 9:16 optional social variant.
- Tone: grounded cyberpunk thriller, tense but not glossy.
- Visual rule: rain reflections should reveal information before characters do.

## Characters

### Mira Vale

- Age: 27.
- Role: delivery rider.
- Wardrobe: yellow rain jacket with torn left sleeve, black cycling gloves,
  dark pants, messenger bag.
- Continuity anchors: silver compass zipper charm, wet hair stuck to cheek,
  left sleeve tear.

### Unknown Figure

- Role: watcher.
- Wardrobe: clear plastic raincoat, face mostly obscured by reflections.
- Continuity anchors: appears only in reflections until the end.

## Locations

### Neon Market Street

- Night street with noodle shop sign, wet asphalt, narrow sidewalk, passing bus.
- Continuity anchors: noodle shop sign, storm drain, bus reflection, blue device
  light reflected in puddles.

## Props

### Memory Device

- Black rectangular device, glass surface, weak blue pulse.
- Must remain small enough to fit in one hand.

### White Envelope

- Sealed, matte white, hidden inside Mira's delivery bag.
- No visible brand or private logo.

## Continuity Watchpoints

- Mira's jacket must stay yellow.
- The sleeve tear must stay on the left sleeve.
- The compass charm should remain attached to the zipper.
- The memory device should pulse blue, not red or green.
- The unknown figure should be visible through reflection or silhouette, not as a
  clear face reveal.
- Rain and wet pavement should remain consistent across shots.
