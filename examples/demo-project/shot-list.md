# Rainline Shot List

This shot list is intentionally simple. Use it to compare a manual plan against
the app's Director pass.

| Shot | Intent | Visual Notes | Continuity Anchor |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Establish the rain-soaked market street. | Wide shot, neon noodle sign, wet asphalt, Mira entering frame with bike. | Yellow jacket, noodle sign, rain reflections. |
| 2 | Introduce the ticking delivery pressure. | Close-up of phone timer at 00:59 in Mira's wet glove. | Black cycling gloves, rain droplets. |
| 3 | Discover the device. | Low angle through storm drain bars, weak blue pulse below. | Storm drain, blue device light. |
| 4 | Make Mira active. | Medium close-up as she pulls the device from the drain. | Torn left sleeve, compass charm. |
| 5 | Reveal tomorrow footage. | Insert of device screen showing Mira one minute later. | Same street, noodle sign, missing bike. |
| 6 | Hint at threat. | Passing bus reflection briefly shows the unknown figure. | Clear raincoat reflection, no full face. |
| 7 | Introduce the message. | Device screen displays `RETURN WHAT YOU STOLE.` | Blue device glow. |
| 8 | Reveal envelope. | Over-shoulder into delivery bag, white envelope inside. | Messenger bag, sealed white envelope. |
| 9 | Commit to action. | Mira pockets device and runs into rain as sign goes dark. | Yellow jacket, blue glow, sign blackout. |

## Suggested First Test

Generate shots 1, 3, 5, and 9 first. That gives the continuity system enough
anchors to compare location, prop, wardrobe, and tone before spending time on
the full sequence.
